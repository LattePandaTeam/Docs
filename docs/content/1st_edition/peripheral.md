## Connect to Peripheral Devices

LattePanda is compatible with a wide range of peripheral devices. You may connect any USB device, such as a flash drive, mouse and keyboard, or webcam to one of the USB 3.0 or 2.0 ports. The SD card socket supports extra storage from a mini SD card. You may also connect the LattePanda to an external speaker device through its 3.5mm audio jack.
